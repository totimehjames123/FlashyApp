Get access to loan quickly with flashy app

Overview

The youth of Ghana who now make up 36 percent of the country's population cannot find jobs, according to the World Bank. These eligible youth are unable to find work due to less job opportunities available in the country.

Moreover, most of the ghanaian youth have skills, great ideas and the willingness to start a business but does not have enough capital to establish their own business.

Lack of access to financial services is a key constraint on the growth of enterpreneurship in Ghana. and the banking sector is complicated and bureaucratic, its processes are time-consuming and not very customer-friendly, which is to the disadvantage of the country as a whole.

For these reasons, team named 'Progression' of the Fintech Hackathon 1.0 has come up with an idea of designing a loan app named 'Flashy app' to be able to help individuals and enterpreneurs in Ghana get access to loan easier and faster, to empower individuals and enterpreneurs to capture financial opportunities in Ghana.
